Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue and restore the lives of stray and neglected dogs and cats — offering them compassion, healing, and a chance to trust again. With dedication and care, we match them with loving families who will provide safety, joy, and a forever home. Because every second chance has the power to create a lifetime of love.

Food Guide

What pets can and can't eat. Always consult a veterinarian for specific needs.

✅ Can Eat

  • Quality wet & dry pet food
  • Fresh water (always!)
  • Lean meats (cooked, unseasoned)
  • Some veggies (e.g., carrots, peas, pumpkin)

❌ Cannot Eat

  • Chocolate, xylitol, grapes/raisins
  • Onions/garlic, alcohol, caffeine
  • Cooked bones (splinter risk)
  • Raw dough, spoiled foods
See more specifics

Every pet is different — always check with your veterinarian if you're unsure about a particular food item, especially for pets with allergies or medical conditions.
